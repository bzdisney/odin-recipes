Poptarts

Description

If you can't make this, give up.

Ingredients

  • Toaster - use the one you have. Don't have one? Ask your mom.
  • Plate - unless you just like holding hot tarts.
  • Poptarts - I won't tell you what kind to get, but if you choose certain flavors you'll probably lose friends.
  • Cup - it's for milk
  • Milk - it's for the cup. And tarts.

Steps

  1. Put Poptarts in Toaster - You want these fools hot but not burnt.
  2. Take Poptarts out of Toaster - Wouldn't make much sense to leave them in the toaster.
  3. Place Poptarts on Plate - or hold them in your hand you peasant.
  4. Pour Milk into Cup - pouring milk on the table won't keep the milk together.
  5. Serve - Dip that steamy rectangle into your milk, then bite it. It's better this way.
